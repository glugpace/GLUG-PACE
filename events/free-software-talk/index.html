Date: 05-04-2020 5pm

Jitsi Meet

With the society constantly changing and producers trying to keep up with the demand, people are being exposed to more number of technological advancements.

These advancements lead to questions regarding how many of these softwares are actually available to the users to study, change, and distribute it and any adapted versions. This is exactly what free software or libre software is.
