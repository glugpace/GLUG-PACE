BELLES-LETTRES

Date: 12-06-2021

Glug Pace 2k21 is hosting Belles-Lettres ( French for essay writing ) which is a year-long event. Any student may participate, irrespective of their branch. The participants will be given a topic each month, and must submit their essays within a certain time limit. Both the topic and time remaining will be displayed in the website. At the end of the academic year, the grades will be added up and the winner will be declared the CHAMPION of Belles-Lettres.

Grading System

Marks will be given based on the following:-

Criteria Marks
Creativity 20
Content 20
Grammar 20
Originality 20
Vocabulary 20
Total 100
