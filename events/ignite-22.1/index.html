IGNITE 22

Date: 18-11-2022

Venue: EduSat Hall

GLUG Pace, Department of Computer Science and Engineering is organizing an event called Ignite '22 on 10th December 2021 at 9am-4pm, in the EduSat Hall.

The agenda of the event is to spread the importance and strategies of open-source development and the value of digital privacy and the consequences on the life of human beings when it is subject to intrusion.

The Speakers are:

Mr Yajnesh Talapady, is a Problem-solving engineer, entrepreneur, and mentor with 7+ years of solid experience in building B2C applications. Skilled in Android/Kotlin/Java. Experienced Software Engineer with a demonstrated history of working in the computer software industry. Skilled in Android application development and Java SDK development. Strong engineering professional with a Bachelor of Engineering focused in Computer Science from PA College of Engineering. IoT enthusiast, have built smart-home, voice-enabled services for few companies.

Mr Sushin P V, is a Senior Tech Lead at upGrad and is Responsible for Technical Product Development, Architecture Design, Product Performance and Scalability at upGrad Jeet, which includes both frontend and backend. will be speaking on Glug's journey and Technologies of future.

Mr Rameez Thonnakkal is a Group Development Manager at Intuit India has experience in working on huge MNC's like Oracle and now Intuit. He will be talking about "Free software and industry".
