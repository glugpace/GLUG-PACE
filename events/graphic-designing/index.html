GRAPHIC DESIGNING WORKSHOP

Date: 31-05-2021 to 01-06-2021

This Workshop was organised by the Department of Computer Science and Engineering in association with GLUG PACE and CSI held on 31st May and 1st June 2021. This online workshop was a successful 3 Hour event conducted by Aadhil Saajid . Around 89 registered for the event and their active participation Made this event a huge Success.

The first session was logo designing, he started with Netflix logo then WhatsApp logo and last one was Glug logo . Lastly, he concluded with a Logo designing competition making it a very interactive session.





Second session was about poster and certificate designing. The software he used was an open-source software Figma promoting glug. The session started with a talk by GLUG PACE president, Muhammed Shayiz KP, about the free software and its benefits. And reaching out to the participants about the events conducted by the group, encouraging them to participate and gain experience.





Towards the end of the session a positive feedback was received and Vice Principal gave a small brief talk on promoting glug and session was ended by hafeez with a vote of thanks.
