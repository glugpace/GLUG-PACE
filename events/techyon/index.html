TECHYON

Date: 15-02-2019

Venue: Multi Purpose Hall

Enlightning the students about the scope of free software, merits of wroking on such projects and make them aware of #TechForDemocracy

SCHEDULE

Introduction to CLOUD COMPUTING and experience at the FSMK camp in the Clod track. Live setp of server time avails.(Handled by Mohammed Shakir and Ahmed Ansad)

Introduction to Data Science and Artificial Inteligence,experience in the AI track at the camp the importance of privacy.(Handled by Mohammed Shayiz K.P, Vishnu Bhagirathan and Sharon M

Side notes:

  • Why share the camp experience

    • One of the main beliefs of the studetns are,only the nerds participate in all such activities.The fun and experience at the camp will to remove stereotypes

SUMMARY

The overall ambition is to increase the participation as well as spreading awarness on privacy and free software philosophy to the community and students

All 3 track speakers presented their topics really well. The session was interactive, specially the part which Shayiz covered was intresting and felt almost everyone understood why they should be a part of GLUG.
