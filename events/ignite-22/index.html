IGNITE 22

Date: 10-12-2021

Venue: EduSat Hall

GLUG Pace, Department of Computer Science and Engineering organised an event called Ignite '22 on 10th December 2021 at 9am-12pm, in the EduSat Hall.

Mr. Shijil, FSMK General Secretary and Mr. Shayiz KP, Alumni from PACE, were the speakers for the event. They have highlighted the importance and strategies of open-source development and the value of digital privacy and the consequences on the life of human beings when it is subject to intrusion. The audience were further encouraged to partake in future events and to integrate these principles of GLUG in their professional and personal space, in order to cultivate personal growth, professional opportunities and to be part of community building.

Dr. M Sharmila Kumari, Vice Principal and Head of the Computer Science department and Dr. Ramis M K, Principal addressed the gathering. Mr. Nazim welcomed the audience, Mr. Nawaf proposed the vote of thanks and Aishwarya, was the Master of Ceremony throughout the session.
