Workshop on OpenCV

Date: 18-05-2021

Glug Pace and ASCII together under Department of Computer Science and Engineering conducted a workshop on Computer Vision OpenCV on 18th May, 2021. This online workshop for 3rd year students was handled by Muhammad Shayiz KP, President of GLUG PACE.

The workshop was a success, with around 70 participants, and the three hours were excitingly interactive. The session covered the topics like Image, Operations on image, what is computer vision and OpenCV, and functions available in that. Three projects made using OpenCV was also shown in the session.
