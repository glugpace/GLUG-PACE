TRYST

Coming Soon

For anyone who want to engage in designing, or showcase your talent in it, GLUG PACE 2k20 is hosting TRYST, which is a year long event. This competition, along with CODATHON and (...), you are welcome to participate, regardless of your branch. Every alternate months there will be a designing contest. Theme for the contest will be either technology related or from current affairs. The topic will be announced (depending on the level of work) prior the submission date and youâ€™ll have to upload it before the due time. The contestants are graded each month based on certain factors, mainly clarity of theme and layout. End of the year, the scores will be summed up and the winner will be chosen as the STAR OF TRYST.

Grading System

Marks will be given based on the following:-
  1. Colour
  2. Typography
  3. Image size/Clarityr
  4. Content
  5. Layout
  6. Clarity Of Theme
  7. Use of FOSS
