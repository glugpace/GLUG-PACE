TechXplore

Think fast. Speak faster.

Category: IntelliTalks

An extempore where participants present impromptu speeches on tech topics

Prerequisites:

  • Communication skill

Rules:

  • Participants will be given a topic on the spot..
  • Topics will cover basic real world trends.
  • Participants will be given 3 minutes with an additional 2 minutes grace time to speak. 2 minutes will be QnA asked by the judges based on the topic recieved.
  • The event consists of only 1 round.
  • In case of dispute, the decision of the judges is final.

Judging Criteria:

  • Content
  • Delivery
  • Spontaneity
