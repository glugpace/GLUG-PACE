Terminal Tussle

Master the command line, rule the machine.

Category: CodeConquer

A CLI-based competition where participants solve challenges using only terminal commands.

Prerequisites:

  • Basic Linux CLI Knowledge

Rules:

  • Mobile Phones and other electronic devices must be handed over to event coordinators.
  • Use of internet is not allowed.
  • Misuse of systems will lead to disqualification.
  • The event consists of 2 round.
  • The commands must first be written in the sheet provided by the coordinators. Completing tasks without filling the sheets will lead to disqalification
  • In case of dispute, the lead coordinator’s ruling is final.

Judging Criteria:

  • Speed
  • Accuracy
  • Use of efficient Commands

Event Type: Solo

Date: 12th May

Time: 09:30 AM – 10:30 AM

Venue: NT Lab
