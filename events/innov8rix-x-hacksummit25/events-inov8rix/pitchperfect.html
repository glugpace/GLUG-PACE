Pitch Perfect

Sell your idea like a pro

Category: IntelliTalks

Teams present an idea with execution plan and pitch to a jury.

Prerequisites:

  • Presentation skill
  • Business sense

Rules:

  • A PowerPoint presentation must be used during the pitch.
  • Each team will have 7 minutes to present their pitch, followed by 3 minutes for Q&A.
  • The event consists of 2 rounds.
  • Teams are not allowed to use external assistance during the preparation.
  • In case of dispute, the decision of the judges is final.

Judging Criteria:

  • Innovation
  • Clarity
  • Feasibility
