Quiz Quiz Quiz!

A quiz was conducted for the students of CS department on 29th March 2020. This fast paced quiz was conducted online on Kahoot! as a fun activity during the quarantine days to get the brains of people up and running.

The quiz contained general, technical as well as coding questions. The top 10 were selected for the next round, and the winners of the 2nd round were awarded.
